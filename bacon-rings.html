Bacon Rings

Price : R60

Serving size : 2


Heat a barbecue grill with hood on medium-high heat. Line a baking tray with baking paper. Place onion rings on prepared tray. Place pineapple rings in centre of onion. Place string cheese, torn, over pineapple. Trim bacon rashers into straight strips and remove rind. Wrap around the onion and pineapple, overlapping, and threading through the centre.
